International Workshop on Biophysics and Soft Matter

26-28 May 2023

City University of Hong Kong

This workshop is organized by the Department of Physics, City University of Hong Kong. In this workshop, we will bring together researchers in various areas of biophysics and soft matter, including but not limited to the structures and interactions of DNA, proteins, and other biomolecules, polymer physics, rheology, colloids, active matter, self-assembly/organization, Monte Carlo simulation, molecular dynamics simulation, single-molecule experiments.
